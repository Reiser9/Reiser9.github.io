Why does Aleph Zero need a testnet?

Testnet is a part of our development process. Initially, new features are deployed to Aleph Zero internal devnet. Later on, they're moved to testnet, where we can battle-test different solutions together with our community.

A powerful, scalableand open source infrastructure layer

Mazze is a public blockchain that has been designed to offer superior speed, security, and scalability. It boasts a DAG-based consensus protocol and an integration with customized Substrate modules. The protocol is also distinguished for being peer-reviewed and garnering academic support.
