The simulation of matches on OnThePitch aims to replicate real football matches as closely as possible.

In contrast to many other football games that rely on calculating results based on players' skills and then generating events, OnThePitch simulates the entire match, and the events of the game (such as passes, shots, crosses, and goals) determine the result.

How does the game's economy work?

The presale is an initial offering of $OTP tokens before they are listed on exchanges. During the presale, early investors can purchase $OTP tokens at a discounted price. To participate in the presale, you will need to visit the Pinksale website and follow the instructions to purchase $OTP tokens. Please note that participation in the presale is subject to certain terms and conditions, which will be listed on the Pinksale website.
