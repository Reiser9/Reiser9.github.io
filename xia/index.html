Token

The IAXIA utility token (IAX) plays a central and dynamic role within the IAXIA ecosystem, facilitating a range of essential functions and interactions. As a utility token, IAX is designed to have inherent value and purpose within the ecosystem, enabling users to access and benefit from various features and services.

Furthermore, we have implemented a unique 2% Burn mechanism. Unlike traditional token burns, our contract permanently destroys tokens, reducing the overall token count, creating a truly deflationary model, and potentially increasing the value of the remaining tokens.

BlockChain

The IAXIA Blockchain is a fundamental aspect of our project, providing a secure and decentralized infrastructure to support the IAXIA Network's growth and functionalities. Our blockchain will be created as a fork of the Ethereum blockchain, which will allow for the migration of current contracts and provide support for today's blockchain developers.

At the core of our blockchain is the IAX20 protocol, which will serve as the foundation for our network's operations. As we progress through the development phase, more details about the protocol and its features will be disclosed to the community.

Explanation of the problem IAXIA aims to solve

Lack of Trust and Transparency

The crypto industry has faced challenges related to scams, fraudulent projects, and lack of transparency. IAXIA aims to build trust and transparency within its community by offering a platform that promotes open communication, verifies project owners through thorough KYC processes

Lack of Secure and User-Friendly Wallets

Security is a critical concern in the crypto industry, and many users struggle to find secure and easy-to-use wallets for managing their digital assets. IAXIA addresses this by providing the IAXIA Wallet and Swap App, which offers a secure platform for storing and exchanging tokens.

Limited Access to Tokenized Platforms

Launching new platforms and applications on the blockchain can be a time-consuming and resource-intensive process. IAXIA aims to solve this problem by offering pre-built, tokenized platforms that can be rebranded and deployed quickly.

Lack of Education

The crypto space can be intimidating for newcomers due to the complex technology and jargon involved. Many individuals are hesitant to enter the market due to a lack of understanding. IAXIA aims to solve this problem by providing a comprehensive crypto education platform.

Complex Project Launching

Creating and launching crypto projects can be challenging and time-consuming, especially for those without technical expertise. IAXIA's LaunchPad utility simplifies the process, allowing users to create and launch various types of crypto contracts on different blockchains
