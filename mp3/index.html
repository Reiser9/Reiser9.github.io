The Shape Canvas

Artists use the shape canvas on OnlyMP3 to create unique pixel paintings, serving as distinct identifiers for their music.

Solution

ONLYMP3 - a vibrant NFT marketplace, where artists and collectors alike can trade audiovisual NFTs. For the first time, artists can monetize their work by visually representing their sounds, opening up a whole new way to appreciate and invest in music.

Through self-directed ownership distribution, artists create opportunities for fans to invest.

Currency

Transactions on this unique platform are exclusively settled using OMP3, the marketplace's own currency, fostering a vibrant and engaging community.

Distribution

Artists have full control over distribution and can sell shares of ownership in the form of NFTs.

Interaction

Fans can view, interact with, and invest in the ownership distribution that artists develop
